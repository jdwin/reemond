WHO AM I?

Contact me

As a cinematographer and time-lapse photographer, I am devoted to capturing scenes that effectively portray the beauty of matter in motion-- I strive to make the viewer feel the pulse of each shot.

Most of the footage on this site is available for licensing, and I am always interested in working on new and diverse projects.

My footage can be seen on: TSN/ESPN, CBC, CTV, E! Entertainment Television, Niagara Tourism, Tourism Toronto, Oxford Properties, Metrolinx, Luminato Festival, Toronto Blue Jays, and major condo developers in downtown Toronto.
